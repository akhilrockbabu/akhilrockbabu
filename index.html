Food for Life

My project, entitled ‘Food for Life', is a dynamic webpage that helps eligible people to receive food from selected hotels without money. This website mainly focuses on people who like to donate, volunteers who ensure the services for eligible people, and poor people who suffers from a lack of food. There are 4 modules: admin, donors, volunteers, and hotels. Here The volunteers can apply for the food card for eligible people. Then admins can view those applications for food cards and can decide to accept or reject them if the application is accepted. The recipient can now have food from selected hotels for free. Donors can donate money as their wish to the admin, and they can also have the receipt for each donation. Admin is the one. who pay the hotels from the received donations. Admin can also view all the received donations and can check the balance of the donations. Admin have all over control of the web.
